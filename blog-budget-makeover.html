Low Budget Home Makeover: Under ₹20,000

By Raj Kumar • February 25, 2026 • 7 min read

Think you need a fortune to transform your home? Think again. Here's a complete room makeover under ₹20,000 with maximum impact.

Budget Breakdown

Item Cost
Fresh paint (one accent wall) ₹3,500
New cushion covers & throws ₹2,000
DIY wall art materials ₹1,500
Indoor plants ₹2,000
New curtains ₹3,000
Lighting upgrade ₹4,000
Total ₹16,000

1. Paint One Wall

Instead of painting the whole room, choose one accent wall. It creates a focal point and costs 70% less.

2. Soft Furnishings Make a Difference

New cushion covers, curtains, and a rug can completely change the room's character without major investment.

3. DIY Artwork

Create your own wall art using canvas, fabric, or even recycled materials. It's personal and pocket-friendly.
