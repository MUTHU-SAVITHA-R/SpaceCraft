Client Success Story

2BHK Apartment, Chennai • Completed January 2026

Before

  • Dark, cramped bedroom
  • Poor storage utilization
  • No visual flow

After

  • Light, airy feel
  • Custom wall storage
  • 40% visual space improvement

The Challenge

Our clients, a young couple in Chennai, had a 2BHK where the master bedroom felt impossibly cramped. A double bed + wardrobe left no floor space, making the room feel like a storage closet.

Our Solution

We redesigned with space-saving custom furniture and a light color palette:

  • Wall-mounted floating wardrobe (visual lightness)
  • Platform bed with hydraulic storage
  • Mirrored wall to double visual space
  • Soft white and pastel mint color scheme
