About SpaceCraft Interiors

Making beautiful homes accessible to everyone through innovative online design consultation.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SpaceCraft Interiors began with a simple idea: great interior design shouldn't cost a fortune. We realized that traditional interior design services were expensive, time-consuming, and often out of reach for middle-class families.

Our founder, Savitha, an IIT Delhi alumnus and interior design enthusiast, created SpaceCraft to bridge this gap. By moving the entire consultation process online, we've cut costs by 60% while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Today, we've helped over 5,000 families across India transform their homes, with an average customer satisfaction rating of 4.8/5.
